Motorola Solutions Summer Internship

Envision School Safety Solution

My Role

During the summer 2018 (6/2018 - 9/2018), I worked as a UX Design Intern in Motorola Solutions - a technological solution provider in the public safety space. I teamed up with my fellow intern (Olivia Kang) working on the school safety project envisioning and designing an end-to-end system solution that promotes a safer school environment. The project was supported by Motorola Solutions User Experience Team and Strategy Team. As a team of two, we focused on exploring problem space, discovering stakeholder and user needs, proposing, prototyping, and refining a systematic solution. My fellow intern and I collaborated closely on the main project efforts and deliverables.

More specifically, I led the efforts in the initial research, mapping and problem definition process. I was in charge of designing system maps and diagrams. I contributed some of the major ideas in the ideation process. I also drew part of the storyboard. I was also in charge of prototyping certain products within the system solution.

Project Overview

Unfortunate events (e.g. active shooter incidents, natural disasters and fires) and undesirable activities (e.g. drug dealing, bullying and vandalism) take place in and around schools. Safety and security are critical for academic institutions and public safety agencies alike. The project aims to envision solutions to improve monitoring and response capabilities, thereby enhancing the resilience and collaborative potential of school stakeholders.

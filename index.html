ABOUT



Hi, I'm Yanling.

I am currently a master candidate studying Human Factors in Information Design at Bentley University. I am an aspiring research-driven Product & UX Designer adept at translating user goals and needs into clear engineering and product specifications.

I am passinate about designing and building experiences that are useful, pleasant and meaningful. To me, good design solutions come from deep empathy of the users, good understanding of the problem, constraints and business objectives, iterations closely with user feedback, and intentional decisions with solid reasoning.

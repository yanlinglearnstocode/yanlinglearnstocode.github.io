Illos

I draw and illustrate quite frequently for both fun and more practice, based off what I see and love. I like digital art, illustration, watercolor painting and logo/icon design. Below is a collection of my recent work.

Welcoming Event Poster

Illustration/Poster Design

Every fall semester, my local student church holds big welcoming events for new students and scholars newly coming to the University of Michigan and Ann Arbor. For consecutive two years, I took the task to design the posters for the pre-event publicity.

Space

Illustration

Inspired by a famous dribble piece by Designer Nina Georgieva, I tried to apply the idea of using recurring shape into my illustration. I made this illustration piece "Space" to depicit my understanding of the infinite space.



Isometric Island

Illustration

Inspired by the famous game Monument Valley, I was personally fascinated by the unique graphic design style of the game, and I recreated this piece a small island in a "Monument Valley" style.


Polaroid

Motion Gaphics

It always seems like a magic to me how you get photos instantly with a simple polaroid camera. Recently I am so obsessed wiith creating motion graphics, though I am still at the stage of learning, imitating and practicing. And here is a small piece!

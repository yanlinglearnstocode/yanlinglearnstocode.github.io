Experienced Data Cruncher, Critical Thinker, New Designer

Hello, I am Yanling Xu.

Currently I do survey sample management, database construction and statistical analysis at Health and Retirement Study in Institute for Social Research - the largest social science institute in the US.
My aspiration for UX design career began with my undergraduate research project on college binge drinking, during which I saw a need to make available solutions to binge drinking more accessible. Since my graduation, I have continued to pursue my interests by searching for and answering to the various real-world demands that my community had for the web and mobile interfaces. As I am passionate to continue building on my interests and the projects I have started, I have decided to pursue graduate studies and to officially start a career as a UX/UI & product designer.

I aspire to design and build experiences that are useful, pleasant and meaningful. To me, good design solutions come from deep empathy of the users, good understanding of the problem, constraints and business objectives, iterations closely with user feedback, and intentional decisions with solid reasoning.

I am currently seeking opportunities to return to school and undertake graduate studies in Human Centered Design and Engineering field.
